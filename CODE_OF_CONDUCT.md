# Code Of Conduct

Elf3D contributors and maintainers are expected to keep project discussion
professional, technical, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Stay focused on the work and the evidence.
- Assume good faith while asking for concrete clarification when needed.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect that users may be unable to share confidential models or customer
  data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private contact information, credentials, customer data, or
  proprietary assets.
- Pressuring users to disclose confidential models or internal project details.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this
code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or
permanent restriction from project spaces.
